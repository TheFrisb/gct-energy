About Us

GREENCORE TRADE(GCT)

GREENCORE TRADE(GCT) is a company focused on the market distribution of electricity generated from renewable energy sources. With the electricity market constantly evolving due to the growing share of distributed generation, there is notable volatility in production and pricing across energy systems. As an aggregator for renewable sources and controllable consumption, GCT offers renewable energy producers a dependable and expert pathway to accessing energy markets.
